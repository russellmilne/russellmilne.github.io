About Research Teaching Hobbies

I'm an applied mathematician, which means that I enjoy using mathematical techniques to solve real-world problems. Most of my work is in the broad field of ecology, with a particular focus on aquatic habitats (e.g. coral reefs, lakes) and spatially explicit problems. I have additionally published a few epidemiological papers. Mathematically, my work incorporates nonlinear dynamics, data science, and machine learning.
