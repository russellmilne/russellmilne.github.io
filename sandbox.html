This is the official website of Dr. Russell Milne. I am a postdoctoral researcher in mathematical biology. Currently, I am a member of the Interdisciplinary Lab for Mathematical Ecology and Epidemiology led by Prof. Hao Wang, in the University of Alberta's Department of Mathematics and Statistics. Previously, I did my Ph.D. under Profs. Chris Bauch and Madhur Anand at the University of Waterloo, and my Master's under Prof. Fred Guichard at McGill University.

If you're interested in my research, you can have a look at my publications on ResearchGate or Google Scholar.
