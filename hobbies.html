When my parents were looking for a new apartment in Vancouver, I created a map of neighbourhoods in the Vancouver area showing them which ones are similar to other neighbourhoods that they like. This map was made by doing PCA on census tract data from Statistics Canada (containing about 2000 features); the first three principal components were encoded as RGB values for each census tract. You can see the map here.
